S.H.E.F. Education Fund.

Founded in 2022 with a secret pilot, In 2024 we are launching the soft pilot, which will run until 2026 when we will officially launch the fund. The SHEF Fund is dedicated to the belief that every child / young adult has the right to free education. Recognising a gap in Ireland's educational system, SHEF aims to support students who do not qualify for the Student Grant Scheme / SUSI due to income being above the threshold. We believe no student should be denied the opportunity to attend university, struggle to attend, go into financial debt to attend university because of financial constraints. â‚¬2 million has been added to the fund in 2024 to fund more students, purchase more student accommodation, and a financial commitment up until 2030 to the fund has been made.

Our Mission

We are committed to ensuring every person has the fundamental right to free education. Our goal is to help students make the most of their lives, achieve their dreams, and become the best version of themselves, without being hindered by financial constraints.

Who Can Apply?

If you do not qualify for a government student grant because your family income is above the threshold, we encourage you to apply to the SHEF Fund.

When to Apply?

We recommend reaching out as soon as you receive your Junior Certificate results. Early application allows us to monitor your progress towards university and provide any necessary support, including pre-university tutoring if needed.

What Happens Once I'm Accepted?

  • University Fees: We cover your university fees directly.
  • Materials and Tools: We provide all necessary educational materials and tools.
  • Accommodation: Free student accommodation is available in Cork, Limerick, and starting in September 2024, Galway. We are also planning to expand to Waterford in 2025, as well as build out the accommodation portfolio in the current cities. We ask only that you respect the property and maintain it well.

Support During Your Studies

If you encounter academic difficulties, we will assess the situation and provide additional support, such as extra lessons or tutoring. We conduct quarterly reviews of your progress to ensure you are reaching your highest potential.

Repayment

We do not require repayment. Your success and effort in reaching your potential is our reward. In the future, if you wish to contribute to the fund, you are welcome to do so, but there is no obligation.

Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. All your data will be kept confidential and will not be shared publicly under any circumstances.

How to Apply

Reach out via the contact form on this site and we will be in touch with you. We will send you a list of documents we will require. Then we will review your application and initiate the due diligence process.

Join us in breaking down financial barriers to education and unlocking your potential with the SHEF Fund.
